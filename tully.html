Tully


Brief
The aim of this project was to create a branding identity for new educate together school located in Cherrywood, Dublin that promotes importance of environment awarness, friendliness, strong sense of community and belonging.

Approach

I've decided to create a unique identity that represents fresh, new style of education. Chosen design answeres the needs for imporatance of educating new generation on importance of environment, while keeping on friendly, soft tone of voice to be inviting to kids.
